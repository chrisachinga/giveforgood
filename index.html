About

Empowering vulnerable communities in Mombasa.

  • 2019

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Give for Good KE (G4G) was founded by Sophia Wabosha and Joyce Muriithi to improve the living standards of vulnerable and marginalized groups in Mombasa County and its enviroments.

  • 2020

    Official Registration

    After registering in 2020, G4G focused its efforts on Mwakirunge in Mombasa County, establishing the Maktaba Mission, a community library at the Assistant Chief’s Camp.

  • 2021

    Expanding Our Impact

    G4G expanded its initiatives, including the Darasa Capacity Building and Hadhi (Dignity) Program, to further support and empower vulnerable communities through education, health, and equality programs.

  • 2022

    Community Support

    Through partnerships and community support, G4G continued to provide essential services such as sanitary towels distribution, food drives, and educational support, reaching hundreds of beneficiaries.

Our Programs

Key initiatives to uplift and support our community.

Maktaba Mission

A community library providing scholastic materials and promoting literacy among children.
Achievements: Information Accessibility, Improved academic performance, Promotion of literacy, fostering a love for reading, Empowerment.

Darasa Capacity Building

Mentorship, entrepreneurship, and financial literacy training aimed at increasing confidence, self-esteem, and personal growth.
Achievements: Increased confidence and self-esteem, Personal growth and development, Expanded networks and support systems.

Hadhi (Dignity) Program

Distribution drives for food, sanitary products, and stationery, along with SRHR sessions.
Achievements: Food drives and sanitary pads distribution for 300+ women and young girls, Mentored 500+ women and girls with SRHR education.

Literacy and Numeracy Initiative

Educational boot camps and library corners for early childhood learning to improve reading and writing skills.
Achievements: Improved reading and writing skills, Increased confidence in learning, Enhanced problem-solving skills, Community support.
